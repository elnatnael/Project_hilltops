About Hilltops Academy

Here at Hilltops Academy, we aim to equip our students for a strong, happy, successful future. A key part of their education is our CHARACTER COUNTS Program, through which the children learn to live through these key values. These are: Trustworthiness, Respect Responsibility, Fairness, Caring and Citizenship.

GOVERNANCE

Hilltops Academy is fully accredited by the concerned organ. The school administrators are visionary, active, skilled and professionally competent. The Administration is comprised of a Managing Director, a Superintendent, Academic Quality Assurances, Principals, Supervisors and others. The school is Consisted of well- trained, certified professional educators. Faculty members are selected from among the best candidates in the highly competitive teaching market. In most cases, our teachers have several years of experience in teaching and are well-aware of the special challenges.

LIFE SKILLS

Our curriculum focuses not only the acquisition of Knowledge, but also on the development of thinking and problem solving, as well as life skills Therefore different extracurricular activities are designed to meet learners personal, social and educational needs. We are moving toward placing each pupil and his or her learning needs at the heart of all that we seek to do. Our job here is to be creative and to help others to be creative too. This life skills program is designed to help students figure out what type of career they want to pursue and how to start preparing for it.

PHILOSOPHY

  • To be recognized by the success of our students in achieving their personal goals.
  • To make Students development the center of all school decisions
  • To aspire to the highest internationally recognized performance standards.
  • To celebrate a culture based on internationalism
  • To build and celebrate, our diversity of cultures, backgrounds and perspective
  • To create a learning environment that will positively contribute to international and local communities.

Our History & Legacy

Founded in 1995, Hilltops Academy has grown from a small community school to a leading educational institution recognized for academic excellence and innovative teaching methods.

1995

Hilltops Academy founded with just 50 students and 5 teachers, focusing on personalized education.

2005

Expanded campus facilities and introduced the International Baccalaureate program, becoming a pioneer in global education.

2015

Celebrated 20 years of excellence with over 1,000 alumni making impacts in various fields worldwide.

2020

Implemented state-of-the-art digital learning platforms while maintaining our commitment to personalized attention.
