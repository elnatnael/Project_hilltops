Welcome to Hilltops Academy

Committed to Excellence in Education

Our Mission

At Hilltops Academy, we are committed to providing quality education and fostering a supportive learning environment for all our students.

Our Vision

To empower students with a holistic, rigorous, and international education for success and to build quality schools that provide opportunities for growth.

Our Values

Integrity, respect, and collaboration are at the heart of Hilltops Academy. We cultivate a community that values diversity and encourages students to express their unique perspectives.

Qualities of Our Students

Critical Thinkers

Our students exercise initiative in applying thinking skills critically and creatively to make sound decisions and solve complex problems.

Communicators

They express ideas confidently in more than one language, including the language of mathematical symbols.

Risk-Takers

They approach unfamiliar situations without anxiety and have the confidence to explore new roles, ideas, and strategies.

Open Minded

They respect the views, values, and traditions of other individuals and cultures, considering a range of points of view.
